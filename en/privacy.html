Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection and Purpose

  • Emopilot stores each user’s CloudKit ID to verify identity and track usage.
  • This applies during both the free trial and after subscription begins.
  • CloudKit ID is retained for up to one year after a subscription ends or is not started.

2. Retention Period and Abuse Prevention

  • To prevent repeated abuse of the free trial, CloudKit IDs are retained for up to one year.
  • This data is used solely to determine access eligibility and is not used for any other purpose or shared externally.

3. Use of the “Advice” Feature

  • When using the Advice feature, the selected emotion and any text input are sent to the AI server.
  • These inputs are not stored on our server and are not used for any other purpose.

4. Data Security

  • All emotional records are saved securely in the user’s iCloud using Apple’s protection framework.
  • We apply encryption and access control measures to protect user data.

5. Cookies and Logs

  • Basic visit data (such as IP address and browser type) may be collected for performance monitoring and is not used to identify individuals.

6. No Third-Party Sharing

  • We do not share or sell user data to any third parties.
  • All data is used solely for app functionality and statistical analysis.

7. User Rights

  • Emopilot does not offer manual data deletion on request but ensures that data is deleted automatically after a defined retention period.

8. Consent

  • By tapping “Agree” and selecting “Free Use” or “Subscribe”, the user is deemed to have accepted this privacy policy.

9. Policy Updates

  • This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be announced on this page.
