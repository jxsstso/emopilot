Subscription & Usage Guide

📌 Free Trial

  • New users can try the app for free for 24 hours after installation.
  • During this trial period, the “Get Advice” feature can be used up to 2 times.
  • Before starting, you must review and agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use by tapping “Agree” and then “Free Use”.
  • By tapping the button, you are considered to have agreed to the terms above.

💳 Subscription Details

  • After the 24-hour free period, you will need to subscribe to continue using the app.
  • Tapping “Subscribe” and completing payment will activate your subscription and start monthly auto-renewal billing.
  • You may cancel your subscription anytime via the “Account Info > Cancel Subscription” menu.
  • Subscribers can use the “Get Advice” feature up to 3 times per day, and 60 times per month.

⚠️ AI Policy Violations

  • If your input contains content that violates our AI usage policy, a warning will be shown and one usage will still be counted.
  • Examples include: hate speech, threats, offensive or inappropriate language.
  • Repeated violations may result in further warnings or temporary restrictions on access.

🚀 After Subscribing

  • After completing your subscription, please fully close and reopen the app to begin using your subscription features.

✅ Consent

By tapping “Agree” and selecting either “Free Use” or “Subscribe”, you are confirming that you agree to all of the terms outlined above.
