Our Philosophy

“The moment you name you emotion and observe it, it begins to lose its power.”
— Daniel J. Siegel, The Whole-Brain Child

Since ancient times, wise people have known how to step back and observe their emotions.
Yet many of us today still find ourselves overwhelmed or consumed by strong feelings.

As a result, we often avoid or suppress emotions, believing that doing so is rational or proper. Sometimes it’s because we find them too much to handle, or external situations force us to.
But emotions arise for valid reasons — and they eventually need to be acknowledged and expressed.
Suppressed emotions can affect both the body and mind, often worsening our emotional state over time.

This app is more than just a journal.
It’s a tool to help you recognize, face, and organize your emotions with clarity.
In a safe and private space, EmoPilot supports you in observing what you feel — honestly and without judgment.

At first, facing or expressing emotions might feel awkward or unfamiliar.
EmoPilot provides a wide range of emotional categories, from basic to nuanced and complex.
Try slowly selecting the feelings that resonate with your current state.

If you don’t find a suitable option or prefer your own language,
you can always add custom emotions.
Use the app as a tool to discover, define, and express your feelings in your own words.

As you select each emotion, you begin to explore your state of mind.
And by considering coping strategies, you take steps toward caring for yourself.

Through the “Get Advice” feature, you can receive objective feedback, support, or the encouragement to face your emotions head-on.

All records are securely saved to your iCloud,
and you can review simple statistics on your moods and responses,
helping you reflect on your emotional patterns and growth.

Through this process of recording and reflecting with EmoPilot, may you become not just aware of your emotions — but the true pilot of your emotional life.
